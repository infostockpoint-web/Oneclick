Common Optimization Tips
  • Use modern image formats (WebP/AVIF), compress and resize images.
  • Minify and combine CSS/JS. Defer non-critical JS.
  • Leverage browser caching and a CDN.
  • Reduce third-party scripts and unused libraries.
  • Preload critical resources and fonts.
  • Avoid render-blocking resources; inline critical CSS.
