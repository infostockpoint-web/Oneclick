Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us.

Last updated: 2025-09-26

Overview

Many tools on One Click Tools run entirely in your browser. When a tool needs to fetch data from the internet, we only request what is necessary to deliver the feature. We aim to minimize data collection and keep things transparent.

Data Processing

  • Local processing: Many tools process your data on-device. We do not receive your inputs.
  • Network requests: For tools that call public APIs or external services, the minimal required data (e.g., URL you enter) is transmitted to those services to complete your request.
  • Contact: If you email us, we will receive your email address and message content.

Cookies & Local Storage

We may use cookies for basic analytics and to remember preferences. Some tools use localStorage to save your settings or history on your device. You can clear this data from your browser settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

Some features may rely on third-party APIs (e.g., currency rates, geolocation). Their privacy practices apply when you use those features. We recommend reviewing their policies if you are concerned.

Security

We use industry-standard practices to secure our website. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Please avoid submitting sensitive personal information unless necessary for a specific tool.

Data Retention

We retain contact emails and essential operational logs only for as long as needed to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Locally stored data on your device remains until you clear it.
